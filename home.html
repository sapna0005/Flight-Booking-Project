Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, visit our website or mobile app, enter your departure and arrival cities, select your travel dates, and choose from the available flights. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other digital wallets. Please check our payment page for a complete list.

3. Can I change or cancel my flight?

Yes, you can change or cancel your flight, but fees may apply depending on the airline's policy. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific details.

4. How do I check in for my flight?

You can check in online through our website or the airline's app, or you can check in at the airport. Online check-in usually opens 24-48 hours before your flight.

5. What should I do if my flight is delayed or canceled?

If your flight is delayed or canceled, please check your email or the airline's website for updates. You can also contact our customer service for assistance.

6. Are there any baggage fees?

Baggage fees vary by airline and ticket type. Please check the airline's baggage policy for specific information regarding carry-on and checked luggage.

7. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via phone, email, or live chat on our website. Our representatives are available 24/7 to assist you.

8. Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer travel insurance options during the booking process. It's recommended to protect your trip against unforeseen circumstances.
